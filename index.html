Our First Game

Stick Together.

An open world survival game.

Stick Together is an open world survival adventure where you play as sticks. Explore, build, and endure — but the world's a lot less hostile when you've got someone to face it with. A game about holding on.

Follow the Journey

Two bad ideas.
One good studio.

We believe the best games come from a specific kind of stubbornness — the refusal to make something forgettable. We're not here to chase trends or ship fast. We're building games that stick with you because they feel like nothing else you've played.

2 Bad Ideas is small, independent, and deeply intentional. Two people. One game. We built this studio because we wanted to make something we'd actually want to play — and we haven't stopped wanting to play it yet.
