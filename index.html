Stick Together

An open world survival game. You're a stick.

Stick Together is an open world survival adventure where you play as a group of sticks. Explore, defend, build and endure — this isn't your world, but you're going to make it home. Just remember to stick together, your more likely to survive with friends.

About Us

Two Bad Ideas.
One Studio.

Great ideas are amazing... but bad ideas are much easier to come by. 2Bad Ideas is built on the belief that through mess and method we can turn a meh idea into... well, a game. Late nights, bad pizza, crossed-out concepts, and too many "what if a stick could..." conversations. We don't promise to do it right... or even particularly well — but we will get it done.

We're not here to pretend we know what we're doing. We kinda suck at building games. We also don't care. This is our canvas and we're making something. We are here to make something worth playing.
